Our Goal

Empowering Healthy Lifestyles

The overall goal of Saroga is to empower healthy lifestyles, especially due to the Covid-19 Pandemic, when many yoga studios and gyms closed down

Our Motivation

• The Covid-19 Pandemic forced the closure of nearly 25% of health studios and 30% of gyms. This has an impact not just on the economy, but most importantly on people's health.

• In fact, as a result of Covid-19, obesity rates have risen by over 40%! We were aware of the issue, and Saroga was our response. Saroga is a website that enables users to participate in and arrange virtual yoga events.

How It Helps

• Saroga benefits its users both mentally and physically. It has been proven that yoga is an easy and fun activity, packed with an abundance of health benefits.

• Another reason that makes yoga an ideal activity during the pandemic is that it's been proven to reduce stress levels. As a result of the pandemic, stress levels in adults have risen nearly 46%, and yoga can be a good way to improve mental health

• Saroga was designed in a user-friendly way, allowing for anyone, regardless of their technology expirence, to participate in yoga events, and ultimately improve their mental and physical health.

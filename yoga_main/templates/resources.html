Introduction

A brief introduction to yoga and it's benefits

  • Yoga is essentially a spiritual discipline based on an extremely subtle science, which focuses on bringing harmony between mind and body. Yoga brings the opportunity to develop a physical relationship between one's inner-being and their abstract nature. Yoga breaks down into individual branches, all which focus on developing a different part of the body and contain different excersises. Learn more about yoga here

  • The origions of yoga can be traced back to the Indus-Sarasvati civilization in Northern India - nearly 5,000 years ago! Yoga was first mentioned in the Rig Veda, an ancient collection of texts containing songs and rituals used by the Brahmans. Up to the late 1800s, yoga was not popular in places other than India. This began to change after Indian Yoga Masters (Yogi) began traveling to western countries to spread the art of yoga. Overtime, yoga was widely adopted in the west and remains one of the most popular fitness & mindfulness methods. Learn more about the origions of yoga

  • Modern yoga consists of a variety of different poses and teqniques. Although different than traditional yoga, modern yoga still shares many similar characteristics of traditional yoga. Modern yoga places an emphasis on mental techniques, postures, concentration, mindfullness, and most importantly, good health, while traditional yoga focused more on the religious/abstract methods. Learn more about modern yoga practices

  • Practicing yoga leads to an abundance of both physical and mental health benefits. Physical benefits of yoga include better flexability, weight loss, increased muscle mass, maintenance of a high metabolism, and many more! Mental benefits of yoga include reduced stress levels, imporoved mental health, and increased concentration. Learn about more health benefits assosiated with yoga practice

  • Believe it or not, it is actually fairly simple to learn yoga! Please don't be discouraged! If you are a beginner, the easiest way to learn is to learn by watching and doing. Saroga offers many features that can help you learn yoga, such as our detailed recouses and guides, as well as our online yoga events. Additionally, you can join our community to connect with other students learning yoga! This is a good video to learn when getting started with learning yoga
